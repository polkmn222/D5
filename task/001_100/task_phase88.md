# Phase 88 Task

## Context

- The Lead list view needed a stronger Salesforce feel, especially around the left-side list-view title block and the right-side search/setup area.
- The Setup control also needed to move beyond simple column toggles and let the single test user create custom list views.

## Goals

- Redesign the Lead list header and toolbar to feel closer to Salesforce.
- Make Setup capable of creating and managing custom list views.
- Support custom view name, source, visible fields, clone, save, and delete in a single-user flow.
- Keep the list stable and regression-tested.
