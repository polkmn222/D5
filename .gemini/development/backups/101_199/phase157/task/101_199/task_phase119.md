# Phase 119 Task

## Context

Messaging needs stronger SMS/LMS/MMS rules, merge-field substitution, and clearer template behavior. The AI Agent also needs cleaner lead fields, bottom-positioned action controls, modal edit/open behavior, and button-based delete confirmation.

## Goals

- Hide SMS subjects in templates and Send Message.
- Apply `{Name}` and `{Model}` substitutions at send time.
- Auto-upgrade over-90-byte SMS to LMS and enforce a 2000-byte cap for LMS/MMS.
- Update AI Agent lead/object actions and selection UX.
- Document the new messaging rules if they were missing.
